How to Play

User vs Computer.

Select your move (before the timer runs out) by clicking your preferred move icon: ROCK, PAPER, or SCISSORS.

There's a shuffle and both players selected moves are displayed. The timer resets and the game continues till the two rounds are played.

Game Rules

Rock crushes scissors, but is covered by paper.
Paper covers rock, but is cut by scissors.
Scissors cut paper, but are crushed by rock.

Scoring: Each round consists of three single matches. Points are accumulated per single match won. Draws do not count towards the score. The player with the highest score at the end of the rounds wins!

Controls: Click on the icons to select your move. Press the spacebar to pause/resume the game or click on the in-game play/pause icons. Hit Enter or P to start the game or click on the play icon.

Good luck and have fun!🔥😜
